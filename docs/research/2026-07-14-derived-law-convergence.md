## The three pieces

- **Smithian Fold Theory of Everything** (github.com/MettaMazza/Smithian-Fold-
  Theory-Of-Everything): "zero free parameters, zero axioms, one fold";
  derives physical constants in exact arithmetic; the engine HALTS the instant
  a fitted or chosen value is introduced.
- **UnisonAI** (github.com/MettaMazza/UnisonAI): a zero-parameter, exact-
  Fraction, per-character geometric engine. No weights, gradients, backprop,
  or floating point in the prediction path. Memory is held orbits (exact
  counts); attention is unit-capacity selection at a forced lock. Measured:
  beats a GPT transformer twin on held-out cross-entropy at character (1.2891
  vs 1.8878) and word (3.1907 vs 3.4292) scale, reading the corpus once
  (~26 s, zero parameters) against 48,000 gradient passes. Thesis: "a large
  part of what training buys is law wearing a statistical costume."
- **Black-Box Decode Toolkit** (omni/benchmarks/INTERPRETABILITY.md): seven
  registered spectral instruments reading structure out of trained weights.
  Findings: the token embedding is the universal law-carrier (11/11 models
  wake, 4B to 1T, 4.4-130x); the loud spectral band IS the function (~150x
  differential damage from deleting it vs the same random budget); training
  writes it early (embedding wakes at step 256, peak ~4000, plateau); training
  data reads back (Gettysburg echoes 9 words verbatim, own corpus ranks
  first); reasoning is a distinct counted signature.

## The convergence (the integration)

The toolkit states its discipline in four rules. They are our four invariants,
one for one, on a different object.

| Black-Box Decode Toolkit | Flywheel credo |
|---|---|
| Register before you measure (SHA-256 hashed, before any spectrum) | Preregistration; the sealed crucible thesis, hashed before the result |
| Every measurement beats a shuffle-null or it isn't structure | An external check decides acceptance; no accept without beating a null |
| Self-tests void bad runs (theorem-forced identities halt the run) | A verifier that cannot fail verifies nothing; oracle_can_fail |
| Negative results recorded in full, scoped exactly | The honest null is first-class; DRIFT ships as DRIFT, no rescue |
| A fitted or chosen value HALTS the engine | No learned model in the accept path (taken to zero parameters) |
| Every number from a committed, timestamped result file, reproducible | Every result carries a receipt a stranger can re-run |

This is not an analogy of convenience. It is the same operation, the reconcile:
perceive an object, check it against a criterion you did not author (a shuffle-
null, an external oracle), carry a re-checkable receipt (a hashed registration,
a proof envelope), and keep the honest null. The flywheel runs it on agent runs
and claims; the derived-law campaign runs it on weight tensors and physical
constants. Each is the other's corroboration: an independent instrument suite,
built by the operator, arriving at the same discipline from the interpretability
and physics direction that the flywheel arrives at from the agent-verification
direction.

## What it changes for our work

- The thesis "correctness comes from the loop, not the model" has a stronger
  sibling: "a large part of what training buys is derived law wearing a
  statistical costume." Where the flywheel keeps the learned model off the
  ACCEPT path, UnisonAI removes the learned parameter from the ENGINE. Same
  direction, more radical radius.
- The interpretability findings (the loud band IS the function; the law lives
  in the embedding; training writes it early) are direct evidence for reading
  and verifying model structure externally, which is the white-box complement
  to our black-box behavioral monitor and canary tripwire. We could not read
  hosted internals; this campaign reads open ones, under registered nulls.
- The recovery itself hardened our perception organ: web_snapshot now flags a
  block/challenge page instead of freezing it as the source, and fetches with
  a browser user-agent. Never again stop at a wall over shared context.
